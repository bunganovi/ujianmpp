01 About Switzerland

Switzerland is a small country located in Central Europe, known for its natural beauty such as the Alps, clear lakes and charming villages. The capital is Bern, while other major cities include Zurich, Geneva and Lucerne.

02 Experience the Magic of Switzerland

Switzerland is not just about the beauty in sight, but also about the tranquillity in feel. Behind the mist of the Alps and the ripples of Lake Lucerne lie stories of peace that can only be understood by those who come to experience it.

In every small village and old bridge, time seems to pass more slowly. Not because it's lagging behind, but because Switzerland teaches us: that true beauty doesn't need to be rushed.

Sometimes, the best trips are not about where you go, but how you feel while you're there.

03 Unique Facts Switzerland

Switzerland is the only country in the world that has enough bunderground shelter (bunker) capacity for its entire population. This is part of a civil defence policy that dates back to the Cold War era.

In Switzerland, there is a law that prohibits people from keeping just one guinea pig or guinea pig because these animals are known to be very social. They must be kept in pairs to avoid loneliness - this shows how serious Switzerland is about animal welfare.

Switzerland uses a national anthem called the ‘Swiss Psalm’, but funnily enough, this song has never actually been officially authorised by national law. This means that technically their anthem is still ‘temporary’ even though it has been in use since 1961.
